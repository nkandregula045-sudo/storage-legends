# Storage Legends - Complete Game Design Document

## THE PITCH

Players buy abandoned storage units and open them to discover random items. Every item has value, rarity, and belongs to collection sets. Players decide to keep items (for collection bonuses) or sell them (for cash to buy better units).

## CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP

1. **Buy Storage Unit** → Choose from available units at current world
2. **Door Opens Dramatically** → Animation and visual effects
3. **Loot Revealed** → Random items displayed with rarity colors
4. **Decide** → Keep (collection progress) or Sell (get cash)
5. **Earn Money** → Build cash for better units
6. **Buy Better Units** → Unlock new worlds
7. **Complete Collections** → Get permanent boosts
8. **Rebirth** → Reset for prestige tokens and multipliers
9. **Repeat** → Endless progression

## CURRENCIES

### Cash
- Main currency
- Used for:
  - Buying storage units
  - Luck upgrades
  - Inventory upgrades
- Earned by: Selling items, completing quests

### Prestige Tokens
- Earned through rebirth
- Used for:
  - Permanent luck multipliers
  - Permanent value multipliers
  - Exclusive units
  - Cannot be spent on temporary upgrades

## LUCK SYSTEM

Players spend cash on luck upgrades. Luck increases chances of finding higher rarity items.

**Luck Level Progression:**

| Level | Mythic Chance | Cost |
|-------|---------------|------|
| 1 | 0.1% | $1,000 |
| 10 | 0.3% | $50,000 |
| 25 | 0.5% | $500,000 |
| 50 | 0.7% | $5M |
| 100 | 1.0% | $50M |

Players always have a **money sink** to spend excess cash.

## REBIRTH SYSTEM

**Requirement:** $1 Billion Net Worth

**Reset:**
- Cash → $0 (start fresh)
- Units → Reset to World 1

**Keep:**
- All Collections (permanent)
- Cosmetics
- Cosmetic upgrades

**Gain:**
- Prestige Tokens (1 per rebirth, more with multipliers)
- Prestige Multiplier +5% (permanent)
- Unlock exclusive cosmetics

## EVENTS

**Hourly Events (players get one at random):**

### Estate Sale
- Rare items appear 2x more frequently
- Duration: 1 hour
- Boost: Rarity multiplier

### Celebrity Auction
- Exclusive cosmetic items drop
- Duration: 1 hour
- Boost: Exclusive drops

### Golden Hour
- All item values doubled
- Duration: 1 hour
- Boost: Value multiplier +100%

## INVENTORY

**Display:** Huge visual grid

Each item shows:
- **Picture/Icon** (rarity color background)
- **Rarity Color** (visual indicator)
- **Value** ($X,XXX format)
- **Collection Name** (if part of set)

Players love scrolling through huge inventories — this is a key retention mechanic.

## SOCIAL FEATURES

### Trading
- **Unlock:** Level 25
- **System:** P2P trading between players
- **Safety:** Trade values must match or player can add cash
- **Cooldown:** 30 minutes between trades

### Leaderboards
- **Richest Player** (total cash)
- **Highest Net Worth** (cash + item values)
- **Most Mythics** (rarity count)
- **Most Collections** (completion count)

## GAMEPASSES (4 Total)

### VIP Pass ($4.99)
- +15% cash from sales
- Auto-Sell Common items
- Extra inventory slots (+50)
- Fast Open (instant animations)
- **NO pay-to-win luck multipliers**

## MONETIZATION

**Non-Predatory Approach:**
- Cosmetics only (no P2W)
- Pass provides QoL, not power
- Trading allows F2P players to compete
- No energy system or time walls
- Progression is skill + time, not money

---

**Target Audience:** Ages 8-18 (simple UI, satisfying collection mechanics)
